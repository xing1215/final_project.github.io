League of Legends Report

Lu Chen, Xing Chen, Ziyang Chen, Nankun Liu, Ziyi Zhao

Motivation and goals

League of Legends is a fast-paced, competitive online game that blends the speed and intensity of an RTS with RPG elements. Two teams of powerful champions, each with a unique design and playstyle, battle head-to-head across multiple battlefields and game modes. With an ever-expanding roster of champions, frequent updates and a thriving tournament scene, League of Legends offers endless replayability for players of every skill level.

The social impact of League of Legends is huge. The average viewership of 2018 League of Legend World Championship Tournament has reached 46.6million and the viewership peaked in the game IG (Invictus Gaming) vs Fnatic with 205.3 million viewers, according to ESC. As the comparison, 111 million people watched the Super Bowl in 2015.

All of our team members are influenced and attracted by this game. It has become an integral part of our lives. As players, we always want to win. But in reality, we fail very often. So the goal of this project is to estimate the determinants to win League of Legends, other than personal technologies and team cooperation with these unmeasurable data.

Initial questions

Initially, we aimed to predict important elements which determine each League of Legends battle’s result only. We intended to deliver our thinkings through modeling and visualization, based on our knowledge obtained in Data Science and Applied Regression classes. Through the process, we developed the interest in figuring out champions with highest wins, picks, bans and rates.
